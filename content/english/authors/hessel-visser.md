<p>Hessel Visser (age 66) has served with SIL in Southern Africa as a translation coordinator and as a Bible translation consultant. He focuses on Khoesan languages and has earned his PhD with a thesis on the challenges and opportunities of translating person, gender and number information from Greek into the Naro language.</p>
