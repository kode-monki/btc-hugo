<p>Olalekan Bamidele is a Translation Consultant with the Bible Society of Nigeria. He holds a PhD in Biblical Studies from the Nigerian Baptist Theological Seminary, Ogbomoso, and an MA in Translation Studies from Africa International University, Nairobi, Kenya. He is passionate about Cognitive linguistics and Biblical Hebrew Poetry.</p>
