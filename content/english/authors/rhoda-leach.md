<p>Rhoda Leach is a translation consultant with Seed Co.  She has an MA from Cambridge University. She and her husband completed the Makonde New Testament in Mozambique with SIL. Rhoda currently works with the Psalms that Sing initiative, writing guides and leading training workshops in Africa and elsewhere.</p>
