<p>Ruthie Wagner is a Translation Development Specialist with Lutheran Bible Translators, working remotely from Minnesota and traveling to Tanzania for training workshops. She also teaches remotely using YouTube, Zoom/Teams, and WhatsApp. She grew up in Bangladesh and served as a Translation Advisor in Sierra Leone with her husband and children.</p>
