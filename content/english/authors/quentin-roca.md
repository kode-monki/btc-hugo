<p>Quentin Roca is head of translation at Xenizo, a French team devoted to Gateway Language translation programs and technology. He teaches ancient languages in France at public universities and theological institutes. He is completing his PhD, on the usage of patristic arguments in 16th Century reformed writings.</p>
