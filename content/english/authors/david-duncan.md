<p>David has served with Wycliffe for 30 years in IT and national partner relations. He currently leads the development of Tabitha (TBTA), a computer assisted, linguistics-based translation system. David advocates for research towards best practices in new AI/NLP methods, seeking enhancement of quality, ownership, and impact of Bible translation.</p>
