<p>Freddy Boswell works as a Senior Translation Consultant for SIL. He has served in a variety of SIL Global leadership roles, including Executive Director/CEO from 2008-2016, and International Translation Coordinator from 2000-2005. His field work has been primarily based in Solomon Islands. He is the managing editor of SIL&#x27;s Journal of Translation.</p>
