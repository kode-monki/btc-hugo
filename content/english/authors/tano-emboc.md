<p>Tano Emboc is a Translation Consultant for the Jesus Film and serves as the Executive Director of Ethnoarts Philippines. He served with Wycliffe Bible Translators and Summer Institute of Linguistics for quite some time as a mother tongue translator and an entity level consultant. He studied Masters of Divinity in Asian Theological Seminary and is currently pursuing his Doctor of Philosophy in Orality Studies in Asia Graduate school of Theology in the Philippines specialising on Bible Translation and Technology.</p>
