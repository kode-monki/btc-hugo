<p>Stephen Watters is SIL's Chief Research Officer and Director of the Pike Center for Integrative Scholarship. He is a Visiting Scholar at the Institute for Studies of Religion, Baylor University. He holds a PhD in linguistics from Rice University, and has various roles in numerous research projects around the world.</p>
