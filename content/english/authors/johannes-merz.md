<p>Johannes Merz is a Senior Anthropology Consultant with SIL Global. He has a PhD in cultural anthropology from Leiden University, the Netherlands, and teaches anthropology at the School of Language and Scripture, Moorlands College in Christchurch, UK. Johannes is based in Benin, West Africa.</p>
