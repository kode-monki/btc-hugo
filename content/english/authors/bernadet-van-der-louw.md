<p>Bernadet van der Louw has a PhD in sign language grammar, she has worked with the Deaf for over 25 years in different parts of the world. Since its start in 2012 she has been with SIL&#x27;s Global Sign Languages Team. Currently she is a linguistics and translation consultant and coordinator working with sign language projects in the Eurasia area.</p>
