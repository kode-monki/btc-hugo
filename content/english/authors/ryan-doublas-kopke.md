<p>Ryan is an Assistant Professor at Canada Institute of Linguistics (Trinity Western University). He designed and teaches a course called Communication &amp; Translation. He also enjoys teaching Philosophical Perspectives in Linguistics, and Semantics &amp; Pragmatics. His first role was serving as a Translation Specialist in Vanuatu.</p>
