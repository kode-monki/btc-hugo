<p>Judy Heath (MA OT, D.Min BT) is a translation consultant and translation coordinator for SIL Chad, where she has worked for over 27 years. She consultant checks for teams in Chad, and trains translators and consultants in Chad and francophone Africa. She has special interests in the OT and dialectical Arabic translations. </p>
