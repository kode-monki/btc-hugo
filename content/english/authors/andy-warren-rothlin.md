<p>Andy Warren-Rothlin is a UBS Global Translation Consultant assisting projects in Chad, Pakistan and the Afghan diaspora. He previously studied European literature, did a PhD on Modality, Reference and Speech Acts in the Psalms (1998) and has published on OT textual criticism, Hebrew grammar, idioms, pragmatics, Arabic script use and Muslim-idiom translation.</p>
