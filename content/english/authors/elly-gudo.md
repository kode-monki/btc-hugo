<p>Elly Gudo serves as a Translation Consultant for Wycliffe Africa and the Jesus Film Project. He has worked with several New Testament translation projects across Africa and has supervised the translation of multiple Jesus Film scripts globally. He holds a Master of Arts degree in Translation Studies from Nairobi Evangelical Graduate School of Theology (now Africa International University).</p>
