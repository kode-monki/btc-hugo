<p>Todd graduated from DIU in 2001. He and Becky have worked with the Bunong in Cambodia since 2003. During this time he has been involved in linguistics, anthropology, literacy, translation, scripture engagement and administration. The Bunong New Testament was dedicated in 2016 and Old Testament translation is in progress.</p>
