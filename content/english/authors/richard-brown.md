<p>Dr. Richard Brown serves as a senior translation consultant in SIL Global, to which he has belonged for 52 years. His PhD is from the London School of Theology. He served in Africa and Eurasia areas, was Associate Director of Eurasia, and was on the SIL Board for 12 years.</p>
