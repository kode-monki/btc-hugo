<p>Manie van den Heever is the Director of Translation, Consultant Development, and Publications with The World for the World. He is also a translation consultant and researcher specializing in the Hebrew Bible and translation. His work explores idioms in Biblical Hebrew and the translation of divine names and titles.</p>
