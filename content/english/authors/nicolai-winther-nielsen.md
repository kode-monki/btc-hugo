<p>Nicolai Winther-Nielsen served as a Professor of Biblical Hebrew and Information Technology in Copenhagen and is now online professor of the Hebrew Bible (<a href="https://theonlineprofessor.org/">https://theonlineprofessor.org/</a>) and researcher at the VU Amsterdam. He is editor of the Free Danish Bible and develops global Hebrew learning for Bible Online Learner (<a href="https://learner.bible/">https://learner.bible/</a>)</p>
