<p>Sharon Merz is a Senior Anthropology Consultant and the Anthropology Coordinator for SIL Global. She has a PhD in social anthropology from the University of Exeter, UK, and teaches anthropology at the School of Language and Scripture, Moorlands College in Christchurch, UK. Sharon is based in Benin, West Africa.</p>
