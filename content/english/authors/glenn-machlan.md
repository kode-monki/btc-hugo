<p>Glenn Machlan grew up in Rockville, Maryland and worked on the Minangali Kalinga New Testament translation in the northern Philippines. He currently lives in Duncanville, Texas, is an editor on Translator’s Notes, and is a mentor on the Bamboo Project, which is doing Translator’s Notes for the Old Testament.</p>
