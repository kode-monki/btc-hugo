<p>Rev. G. B. Amar is a linguist, Bible teacher, musician, NOBO Bible translator, serving as Project Coordinator of TILL India. He founded Araadhak India Ministries, which promotes EthnoArt initiatives, media, multicultural worship, and runs a music school, fostering creative expressions of faith across diverse language communities. <a href="http://www.araadhak.org">www.araadhak.org</a></p>
