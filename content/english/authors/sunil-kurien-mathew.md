<p>Sunil Kurien Mathew has been involved in Bible Translation for the past thirty-two years. He worked as a cross-cultural Bible translator in a project in Western India and published the New Testament. In 2009, he was certified as a Translation Consultant and since then has been serving as a consultant and trainer for several Bible Translation projects in India. </p>
