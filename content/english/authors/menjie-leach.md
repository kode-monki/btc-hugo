<p>Benjie is a translation consultant with Seed Company and has a doctorate in African linguistics from Leiden University. He has worked for the last four years with the Psalms that Sing initiative in which he is involved in training and in developing workshop guides for some of the individual psalms, including 3 of the acrostics.</p>
