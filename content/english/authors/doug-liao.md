<p>Doug Liao is a PhD Candidate at Fuller Seminary (Pasadena, CA), studying the history of the earliest translations of the New Testament into Siamese (Thai) in the nineteenth century. He lives with his wife, Joy, on the West Coast of Canada.</p>
