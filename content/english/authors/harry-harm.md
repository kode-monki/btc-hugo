<p>Harry Harm is a senior translation consultant with SIL and also is a consultant for anthropology, scripture engagement and innovation and strategic planning.  He has consulted with language groups in many parts of the world and now works mainly with Deaf people as part of SIL's Global Sign Languages Team.</p>
