<p>Chris has been involved in missions work for 25 years. He is a CiT working on projects in Australia and south-east Asia. He has master&#x27;s degrees in applied linguistics and theology with special focus on OBT, cognitive linguistics and the Septuagint. In addition, Chris is a worship leader and regularly teaches around the world on the Bible, worship, missions, and discipleship. He is married with four kids.</p>
