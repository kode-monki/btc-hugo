<p>Dogara Ishaya Manomi holds a PhD in New Testament studies from the University of Mainz, Germany, and an MA in Linguistics and (Bible) Translation from the Theological College of Northern Nigeria, where he has been a lecturer since 2014. Dr. Manomi is a Humboldt Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the University of Frankfurt, Germany. </p>
