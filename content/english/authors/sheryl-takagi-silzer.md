<p>Sheryl Takagi Silzer has worked as a Bible translator in Latin America and the Pacific. She also served as a multicultural consultant facilitating cultural self-discovery for multicultural teams with various mission agencies, schools, and churches. She has published books and articles on multicultural teams and Asian culture.</p>
