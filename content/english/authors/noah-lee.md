<p>Noah Lee, a member of GBT/WGA (1986- ) and SIL Global (1987-2024), served on a translation project in the Caucasus as a linguist-exegete and project coordinator until the publication of the entire Bible. As a translation consultant, he has served dozens of teams and taught Old Testament translation.</p>
